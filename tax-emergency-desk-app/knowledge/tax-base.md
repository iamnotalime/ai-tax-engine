# Tax Desk Knowledge Base v1

## SP2DK workflow boundary

SP2DK response packs should summarize visible facts, requested data, missing documents, and draft a factual response. Do not guarantee acceptance, do not compute deadlines without received date, and route uncertain cases to professional review.

## Coretax credential policy

The product must not collect or store DJP/Coretax username, password, passphrase, or other credentials. Users upload screenshots, XML, CSV, faktur, and supporting documents themselves.

## Evidence matrix standard

Every material statement in a draft response requires source references to uploaded documents. If documents are missing, mark the evidence item as missing or insufficient rather than inventing content.
