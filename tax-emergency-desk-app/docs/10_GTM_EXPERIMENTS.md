# 10 — GTM Experiments for Ads/SEO Bootstrap

This file is for acquisition and product experiments. It is not a substitute for product/security specs.

## Positioning

Primary positioning:

> Dapat SP2DK atau error Coretax/e-Faktur? Upload surat/screenshot, dapat ringkasan masalah, checklist dokumen, dan opsi response pack yang direview profesional.

Do not position as:

- guaranteed tax problem solver;
- official DJP service;
- autonomous AI tax consultant.

## Landing pages

### Landing page 1 — SP2DK

Headline:

> Dapat SP2DK? Susun tanggapan dan dokumen pendukung lebih cepat.

Subheadline:

> Upload SP2DK, dapat ringkasan isu, checklist dokumen, dan opsi response pack yang direview profesional.

CTA:

> Cek SP2DK Gratis

Trust note:

> AI scan bersifat ringkasan awal. Paket kasus spesifik dapat direview oleh profesional/konsultan pajak. Tidak ada jaminan hasil.

### Landing page 2 — Coretax/e-Faktur Error

Headline:

> Bingung error Coretax atau e-Faktur?

Subheadline:

> Upload screenshot/error file, dapat diagnosis awal, checklist field/dokumen, dan langkah perbaikan yang bisa dibahas dengan tim pajak Anda.

CTA:

> Cek Error Gratis

### Landing page 3 — Marketplace Seller Tax

Headline:

> Seller marketplace bingung rekap omzet, fee, refund, dan pajak?

Subheadline:

> Upload settlement report, dapat ringkasan omzet/fee/refund dan file siap dibawa ke konsultan pajak.

## Pricing experiments

### Experiment A — Low friction

- Free scan: free.
- Issue summary: Rp99k.
- Reviewed response pack: Rp1.5m–3m.
- Complex case: quote.

### Experiment B — Premium trust

- Free scan: free.
- Reviewed response pack: Rp3m–7.5m.
- Urgent review: +50%.
- Consultation: quote.

### Experiment C — Marketplace seller volume

- Monthly report: Rp99k–299k.
- Human tax review: Rp1m–3m.

## Funnel events

Track:

- page view;
- CTA click;
- email submit;
- document upload;
- scan completed;
- paid pack selected;
- payment completed;
- case delivered;
- outcome recorded.

## 14-day validation plan

### Day 1–3

- Build landing page.
- Build manual upload intake.
- Prepare sample free scan output.
- Get 1 senior consultant willing to review paid cases.

### Day 4–7

- Launch small Google Ads campaigns for SP2DK and Coretax error.
- Budget small; separate keywords by intent.
- Manually process uploads.

### Day 8–14

- Measure cost per qualified upload.
- Offer paid reviewed pack to qualified cases.
- Interview 5 users.
- Identify most common issue types.

Validation target:

- 50+ leads;
- 10+ document uploads;
- 3+ paid conversions;
- at least one case over Rp1.5m.

## Trust assets to build early

- About page with clear limitations.
- Reviewer verification page.
- Data privacy page.
- Sample redacted response pack.
- FAQ: “Apakah ini resmi DJP?” Answer: no.
- FAQ: “Apakah dijamin diterima KPP?” Answer: no.
- FAQ: “Apakah AI menggantikan konsultan pajak?” Answer: no.

## Content/SEO moat

Build pages for long-tail issues:

- What is SP2DK?
- How to prepare SP2DK response.
- SP2DK checklist.
- Common Coretax e-Faktur errors.
- NITKU and faktur pajak issues.
- Marketplace seller tax checklist.

Each page should route to upload/free scan.
